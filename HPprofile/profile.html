About Me

I am a Psychologist who provides Individual and Group psychotherapy to adults and couples. I am an Assistant Clinical Professor in the Department of Psychiatry at the Berlin School of Medicine and am Past head of the New York City Cognitive Behavioral Therapy Association. I have more than 8 years of experience helping individuals just like you overcome their anxieties, depressions, phobias, relationship problems as well as manage their anger and stress issues.
